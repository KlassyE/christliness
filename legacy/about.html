Our Story, Our Values, Our Commitment

Our Mission

To provide an excellent christian education for children to please Jesus in all their interactions with man (2 Timothy 2:15)

Our Vision

To nurture children into Bible based individuals who are excellently equipped spiritually, emotionally, academically and practically for every good work (2 Timothy 3:16)

Core Values

Excellent spiritual, emotional, practical and academic learning

Meet Our Principal: Kabagambe Ruth

Mrs. Kabagambe Ruth is a dedicated educator with a passion for fostering a learning environment where Christian values and academic rigor go hand-in-hand. With years of experience in educational leadership, she is committed to the holistic development of every student at Christliness International School. Her vision is to see each child flourish, equipped not only with knowledge but also with strong character and a heart for service. She believes in a collaborative approach, working closely with teachers, parents, and the community to create a supportive and inspiring school family.

"Our goal is to raise up a generation that loves God and uses their talents to make a positive impact on the world." - Kabagambe Ruth

Our Curriculum: CLASS & ACE

Christian Liberty Academy School System (CLASS)

The Christian Liberty Academy School System (CLASS) offers a comprehensive, Christ-centered curriculum. It is designed to provide a solid academic foundation with a focus on biblical values, character development, and individualized learning paths to help each student achieve their full potential.

ACE (Accelerated Christian Education)

ACE provides a Bible-based, individualized curriculum that allows students to learn at their own pace. It focuses on character building and mastery of academic concepts, preparing students for future success with a strong moral foundation.

Our Campus & Facilities

Located in the serene environment of Kirinya, Uganda, our campus offers a conducive atmosphere for learning and growth. We provide well-equipped classrooms, a library, science labs, recreational areas, and secure boarding facilities for both boys and girls. Our commitment is to offer a safe and stimulating environment for all students.
